**ATS Resume Optimization Prompt**

Created and tested an AI-powered prompt focused on improving resume quality for ATS systems and recruiter readability. The task involved designing a structured approach to analyze resumes, generate ATS scores, optimize sections, improve keyword usage, and create a professional one-page resume format.

This project helped me understand how prompt engineering can be applied to real-world career solutions by improving resume structure, enhancing clarity, and aligning content with industry expectations while maintaining accuracy and avoiding false information.

Through this task, I explored the role of AI in professional documentation, recruitment workflows, and career preparation processes.
