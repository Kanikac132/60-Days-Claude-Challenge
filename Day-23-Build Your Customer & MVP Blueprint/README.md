# Day 23 – Customer & MVP Blueprint

## Overview

This project documents the Customer & MVP Blueprint created as part of the 60 Days AI Challenge. The objective was to transform a startup idea into a customer-focused product strategy using AI.

## Topics Covered

* Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
* Buyer Persona
* Top Customer Pain Points
* Customer Journey Mapping
* Buying Triggers & Objections
* MVP Recommendations
* MoSCoW Prioritization
* Pricing Hypothesis
* Risk Assessment
* Founder Action Sheet

## Key Learnings

* Customer research is the foundation of successful product development.
* Defining an MVP helps prioritize features that deliver maximum value.
* Understanding user pain points improves product-market fit.
* Structured validation reduces business and product risks.
* AI can accelerate startup planning and decision-making.

This repository includes the generated report, supporting screenshots, and a summary of key insights from the exercise.
