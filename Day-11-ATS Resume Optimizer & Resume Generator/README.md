# Day 11 – ATS Resume Optimizer

## Overview

Today I used AI to analyze and optimize my resume for a **Data Science Engineer** role. The process involved comparing my resume against a target Job Description, identifying missing skills and keywords, and generating an ATS-friendly version.

## What I Did

* Uploaded my resume
* Used a Data Science Engineer Job Description
* Performed ATS analysis
* Reviewed the ATS Match Score and Gap Analysis
* Generated an optimized ATS-friendly resume
* Saved screenshots of the analysis and optimized resume

## Key Learnings

* Understood how Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) evaluate resumes.
* Learned the importance of matching relevant keywords with the target job description.
* Identified skill gaps and opportunities to improve my resume.
* Improved my understanding of recruiter-friendly resume formatting.
