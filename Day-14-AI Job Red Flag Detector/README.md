# Day 14 – Job Red Flag Detector using Claude AI

## Overview

On Day 14, I used Claude AI to analyze a Job Description (JD) along with company information to evaluate the quality and reliability of a job opportunity.

## Tasks Completed

* Collected a Job Description (JD)
* Gathered company information
* Used the Job Red Flag Detector prompt in Claude
* Generated a complete risk analysis report
* Reviewed the overall risk score
* Identified red flags and positive signals
* Analyzed the detailed risk breakdown
* Reviewed the final hiring verdict
* Explored AI-generated interview questions based on the role
* Documented screenshots and key learnings

## Key Learnings

* Learned how to evaluate job opportunities beyond salary and job title.
* Understood how AI can identify potential hiring risks from a JD.
* Discovered the importance of researching companies before applying.
* Learned to recognize positive hiring signals and common red flags.
* Explored how AI can generate relevant interview questions for better preparation.

This exercise demonstrated how AI can support smarter career decisions by helping candidates assess opportunities before submitting applications.
