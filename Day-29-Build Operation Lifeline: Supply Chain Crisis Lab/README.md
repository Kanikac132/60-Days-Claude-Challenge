# Day 29 - Operation Lifeline Simulator

## Overview

For Day 29 of the AI Challenge, I developed an interactive **Operation Lifeline Simulator**, a React-based application focused on supply chain crisis management and strategic decision-making.

The simulator places users in an executive role where they analyze supply chain challenges, make crisis response decisions, negotiate with suppliers, and evaluate AI investment strategies.

## Features Implemented

* Random company profile generation
* Dynamic supply chain crisis scenarios
* Crisis response action selection
* Supplier negotiation rounds
* CEO leadership decision questions
* AI supply chain investment selection
* Executive dashboard with final business scores
* Scenario replay functionality
* Interactive decision-based simulation

## Technologies Used

* React
* HTML
* CSS
* JavaScript
* AI-assisted development using Claude

## Learning Outcomes

* Learned how to build interactive simulations using AI assistance
* Understood supply chain risk management concepts
* Explored decision-making systems and business analytics
* Improved understanding of React-based application development
* Learned how different strategic choices impact business outcomes


## Conclusion

This project demonstrated how AI-powered simulations can help users practice strategic thinking, crisis management, and business decision-making in a risk-free environment.
